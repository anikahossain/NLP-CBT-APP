Mission Statement

Envision is an online resource for those who are interested in learning techniques taken from Neuro Linguistic Programming and Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, to counteract hindrances such as issues with mental health and personal development in order for the user to achieve and fufill their full potential. It is an app which aims to inspire the user to self-learn and track one's progress in regards to personal development. This app includes resources about subjects such as goal-setting, procrastination, as well as anxiety, depression, stress, and intropersonal skills.

What is NLP?

NLP is a method of influencing brain behaviour (the "neuro" part of the phrase) through the use of language (the "linguistic" part) and other types of communication to enable a person to "recode" the way the brain responds to stimuli (that's the "programming") and manifest new and better behaviours. Neuro-Linguistic Programming often incorporates hypnosis and self-hypnosis to help achieve the change (or "programming") that is wanted.
